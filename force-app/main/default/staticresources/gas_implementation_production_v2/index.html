Exterior water service lines can fail due to normal aging and corrosion, but even newer water pipes can leak and break due to extreme temperatures and settling/shifting ground. Help protect yourself against the high cost of repairing and/or replacing your home's exterior water line.

 

For just $5.99/month, the Exterior Water Service Line repair plan gives you:

  • Up to $7,000 of coverage per call
  • 24-hour repair hotline with priority status
  •  Local, licensed and insured contractors
  • Multiple service calls
  • One-year guarantee on repairs

 

Excludes thawing of frozen pipes and branch lines. Additional exclusions apply. See terms and conditions.
